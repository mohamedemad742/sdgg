what is adidas

Adidas is a famous German company that manufactures shoes, sportswear, and all other sports goods and supplies. Its headquarters are located in the German district of Herzogenaurach. At the beginning of the twenty-first century, Adidas was ranked as the largest sportswear manufacturer in Europe and the second largest company in the world after Nike . [ 1] Adidas was founded in 1924 by the brothers Adolf Dassler and Rudolf Dassler, but they did not stay in harmony for long, so they divided the company into two separate companies, Adidas and Puma , in the 1940s.
